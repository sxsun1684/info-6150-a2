About the Library

Vallejo Public Library stands as a cornerstone of civic life in California’s South Bay. For generations, residents have turned here for authoritative collections, quiet scholarship, and programs that elevate the whole community. We uphold a lasting promise of the public library tradition—free and equal access to knowledge—offering print and digital resources, dignified study spaces, and lifelong-learning events from children’s storytime to public lectures.
